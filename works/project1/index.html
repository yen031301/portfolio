CONTEXT

I was tasked to create a modular structured poster for the SAIC Spring 2022 Architecture Lecture Series.

CHALLENGE

Students ideate a conceptual plan. Then build out a well-conceived modular system that allows for the dynamic arrangement of information within a standardized spatial structure. Students experiment with modular systems to organize, emphasize and clarify a complex information set.

SOLUTION

I created a harmonic architectural space constructed with dynamic types and shapes. This design invites the audience into an energetic space and showcases each architect's iconic design. A gradient palette was chosen to enhance the sense of spacial depth.
