CONTEXT

David Carson is a renowned American Designer who advocates for freedom and authenticity. Carson follows his own design philosophy: "Never use guidelines," promoting creative freedom, self-expression, and aesthetic instinct. This poster is a mock promotion for his workshop.

CHALLENGE

Create a poster inspired by a chosen designer. Come up with a concept and utilize type and imagery that expresses the style of the designer.

SOLUTION

The poster conveys fearlessness and adventurous nature inspired by David Carson's Design. The type creates a pathway and mimics the behavior of a map, guiding the viewers' attention.

DELIVERABLES

Poster (18"x24")

BEHIND THE SCENE

I created a harmonic architectural space constructed with dynamic types and shapes. This design invites the audience into an energetic space and showcases each architect's iconic design. A gradient palette was chosen to enhance the sense of spacial depth.
