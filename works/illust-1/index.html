1. TAIWAN: MYTHS & FOLKLORE

[2021] – 5 Weeks

Growing up in Taiwan, I often hear stories from elders about local monsters and mythical beings. But compare to other places, I noticed there are very little illustrative interpretation of these folklores. Therefore, I became interested in reimagining the appearance of the local mythical creatures and turned my drawings into a series ​ Mixed Media (Paper, Ink, Digital Media)
