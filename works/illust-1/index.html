CONTEXT

Chirle Brown is a bakery established in Taiwan since 2003. The bakery adheres to the purpose of bringing joy with their tasty baked goods. Insists on using simplest ingredients and pure handwork in the making. Consistently delivers simple yet delicious and innovative baked goods.

CHALLENGE

I combined Chirle Brown's best-selling product, egg pudding, with their core values in the logo. I designed a mascot as the logo mark to evoke friendliness and give a stronger impression. The color palette is inspired from their past logos.

SOLUTION

The poster conveys fearlessness and adventurous nature inspired by David Carson's Design. The type creates a pathway and mimics the behavior of a map, guiding the viewers' attention.

DELIVERABLES

Logo/ Graphic Elements/ Packaging

BEHIND THE SCENE

I created a harmonic architectural space constructed with dynamic types and shapes. This design invites the audience into an energetic space and showcases each architect's iconic design. A gradient palette was chosen to enhance the sense of spacial depth.
